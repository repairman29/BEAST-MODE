## ðŸ” Review Checklist

Before publishing any marketing material, ask:

1. **Is it true?**
   - Can we prove it?
   - Have we tested it?
   - Is it accurate?

2. **Are we overselling?**
   - Are we making promises we can't keep?
   - Are we hiding limitations?
   - Are we being realistic?

3. **Are we being honest?**
   - Do we mention limitations?
   - Do we admit when we're not perfect?
   - Are we transparent?

4. **Can we back it up?**
   - Do we have proof?
   - Can users verify it?
   - Is it testable?

5. **Would we say it to a friend?**
   - Would we recommend it honestly?
   - Would we mention the downsides?
   - Would we be comfortable with it?
